PlayStation™Vue is #1 in customer satisfaction.*

Get live sports, news, movies, and more with a powerful DVR and On Demand programming, all with no annual contract and no surprise fees. Download the app and start your free trial today.**

Features:

  • Live TV: must-watch shows, sports, news, movies, and more
  • Favorite shows and entire channels can be saved to your own personal cloud DVR - with no recording conflicts†
  • Programming from tons of channels, including AMC, Disney, ESPN, HBO®, NFL Network, and more!
  • Live, DVR, and On Demand programming all part of one seamless experience
  • Personal profiles offer personal DVR and tailored recommendations
  • You and your family can stream on up to 5 devices at once‡
