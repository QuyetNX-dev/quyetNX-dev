Inside Look: PlayStation4 Features

Discover all of the amazing connectivity
and personalization features that make PlayStation4
the destination for the best gaming and
entertainment experiences.

PSN Account

Set up your user account to connect to PlayStation Network and access the PlayStation 4 system’s robust online features. Customize your account with a unique avatar, and tie it to your Facebook account to share your profile picture and name with your friends list. You can also change your profile’s background image and color scheme with a screenshot from your favorite game, or create your own custom dashboard theme. Use your PSN account on the PS4 system, on the web portal for the PlayStation Store, and with the PlayStation App on your mobile device.

Quick Menu

Press and hold the PS button to bring up the customizable “quick menu.” Here you can access useful settings, such as system notifications, friends list, sound and device settings, Share Play, Spotify music playback settings, power and restart options, and more.
