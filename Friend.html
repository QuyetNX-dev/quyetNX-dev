PS Plus: 14-Day Trial

Sony Interactive Entertainment LLC

59 Ratings

Description

This trial gives you full access to PlayStation Plus for free for 14 days. At the end of the free trial period, your subscription will begin and a recurring subscription fee of $9.99 will be deducted from your wallet every 1 month. If your wallet does not have enough funds to cover the subscription renewal, the system will bill your credit/debit card. You can cancel your subscription prior to the end of the 14 day trial and you will not be charged. To cancel your subscription or change your wallet settings, go to [Settings] > [Account Management] > [Account Information].
